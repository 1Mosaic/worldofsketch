Synopsis

In a world where cartoons, known as Sketches, are created to cure loneliness, these companions exist to serve and are believed to be indestructible.

They're not supposed to die.

But when Sara's cartoon boyfriend is brutally murdered, that belief shatters — turning our childhood memories into nightmares.

As fear spreads, Sara is pulled into a surreal, disturbing investigation where what once made us laugh (cartoon anvils, dynamite, and toxic waste) is no longer harmless, but lethal.

Someone has found a way to kill what was never meant to die.

And this is only the beginning.

This film will embody the beloved classic slasher/horror elements of Scream, intertwined with a fresh fantastical world similar to A Nightmare on Elm Street — where anything is possible. This live-action mixed with animation film has a visual style of Who Framed Roger Rabbit, Cool World & Space Jam. The dark style of animation is going to take your idea of cartoons and their deaths to a whole other demented dimension that has never been seen in this genre.

Attached Talent

Emma Roberts

Emma Roberts is an American actress and producer known for her versatility across film and television, seamlessly balancing comedy, drama, and horror. She gained early recognition for her breakout role in Unfabulous and later became a standout presence in projects like American Horror Story and Scream Queens. The Team

Director / Writer

Colton Tran

Colton Tran is an award-winning film director known for crafting visually striking, genre-driven stories. He has directed multiple feature films, including psychological thrillers and horror projects, showcasing a strong command of tone, tension, and cinematic style. His work blends elevated storytelling with commercial appeal, establishing him as a distinct voice in modern genre filmmaking.

Producer

Jeffrey Reddick

Jeffrey Reddick is an American screenwriter and producer best known as the creator of the Final Destination franchise. His work is rooted in high-concept horror, often blending suspense, fate, and inventive storytelling. Reddick has become a respected voice in the genre, contributing to both film and television projects.

Producer

Lance Bass

Lance Bass is an American singer, actor, and producer best known as a member of the iconic boy band NSYNC. After the group's massive success in the late '90s and early 2000s, he expanded into television, film, and radio hosting.
